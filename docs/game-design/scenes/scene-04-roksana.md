## Structural Role

Scene 4 is the game's "far country" — the place the Prodigal Son went when he left home. In narrative terms:

- The player has just been a child at the lake with their father (Scene 3)
- Now they are an adult in a world that is strange, cold, corporate, displaced
- Something in this world leads them back toward home — a moment of recognition, a failure, an exhaustion, a desire

The arc of this scene ends with the player turning toward home. The transition to Scene 5 is the son "coming to himself" after the far country — choosing to go back.
